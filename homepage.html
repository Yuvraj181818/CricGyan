T20 World Cup 2026

The ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026 is set to be one of the most exciting editions yet, running from February 7 to March 8, 2026, co‑hosted by India and Sri Lanka. A total of 20 teams will compete across several venues, including major stadiums in Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Colombo and more.

Nepal has qualified for the tournament and will be placed in Group C, facing strong sides such as England, West Indies, Bangladesh, and Italy. Nepal's first match is scheduled for February 8 in Mumbai, marking their third appearance in the T20 World Cup after 2014 and 2024.

India, the defending champions from the 2024 edition, will kick off their campaign against the United States in Mumbai on February 7. As one of the favorites to lift the trophy, India's group includes traditional rivals and emerging teams, setting up thrilling contests early in the tournament.

Fans across the world are eagerly awaiting the Super 8s and knockout stages, which promise high‑intensity matches and potentially classic T20 battles on the road to the final in early March.
